Virtual key codes
=================

Symbolic constant Hexadecimal value Mouse or keyboard equivalent 
VK_LBUTTON  01  Left mouse button  
VK_RBUTTON  02  Right mouse button

VK_MBUTTON  04  Middle mouse button on a three-button mouse  
—  05–07  Undefined
